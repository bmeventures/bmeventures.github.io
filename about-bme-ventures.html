ABOUT US

BME Ventures

Manufacturing and Sale of Play Equipment, Home Improvement and Quality Life Products

VISION

To enhance quality of life through innovation and play. To assist all children grow to become adults who are better socially, emotionally, physically and healthy through play.


MISSION

To design, manufacture and provide products and services that attempt to meet the huge expectations of childhood and choosy adulthood. To create play spaces in every basic school in northern Ghana and then beyond.


I am one for innovation and creativity. I am very much convinced that play is essential for the proper development of every child into adulthood. Screens are capturing our generation and beyond and it is about time we decide on a shift that will impact generations to come. That is my motivation to do what I do. I have my part to play and so do you.
