The Case for Play (Outdoor Option)

10
Shares

When kids play outdoors, they get the opportunity to encounter challenging situations, get to terms with their immediate surroundings, interact with nature and other kids and of course have fun! Not to mention the well-known benefits to their health such as getting some sunshine (vitamin D) and fresh air.

Below are three major ways outdoor play is very good for children. Note that benefits of outdoor play are so many but find below just a few to get us thinking as adults and leaders.

#1 Outdoor Play Increases Physical Development

  • Muscle Strength and Coordination
  • Gross motor skills
  • Better balance
  • Development of senses



#2 Outdoor Play Builds Social And Emotional Skills

  • High interaction skills
  • Good communication skills
  • Good Imaginative possibilities
  • Respect for nature as an adult
  • More happier children and adults



#3 Outdoor Play Leads to Better Health

  • Burn more calories (reduces obesity)
  • Prevention of weight-related illnesses like type two diabetes, high blood pressure, and heart disease.
  • Boosted immunity
  • Exposure to sunlight results in a good dose of Vitamin D
  • Less stressed children

Parents, invest in quality play for your children for their brighter future.
